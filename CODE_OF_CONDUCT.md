# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be constructive and professional.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and focus on technical facts.
- Accept feedback and correct mistakes quickly.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, personal attacks, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and related project communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for violations.

Report concerns privately to repository maintainers through GitHub's private security/advisory channels.
